    Game Design


    My undergraduate degree is in Game Design & Development with an Art & Design Concentration. I love games of all types, but my primary interest is empathy building through play. Video games are the penultimate art-form, combining many different disciplines. The best examples of games bring harmony to sound design, psychology, computer science, art, and more.

    Projects

    Withered Ash

    Alone to wander an abandoned space station, Dr. Gale Marx must discover the mystery behind the disappearance of thousands aboard the S.S. Rebirth. The black ash that clings to every surface must somehow be involved. She must make a tough decision: destroy the station or try to salvage years of research.

    Little Sprouts

    There is always something new to learn when you're a parent - especially if it's your first time. Take on the role of a parent who is discovering the world of early childhood development with their newborn baby. Determine what seemingly unassuming objects are actually dangerous to children of different ages.

    All World Wheelchair

    Nothing is easy when your business is in the red. Luckily the Big Boss at All World Wheelchair knows exactly how to get the operation running at peak performance. Learn how to problem solve and utilize modern manufacturing techniques to save a floundering factory.

    Neon Pheonix

    "Darko", "Smiles", and "Jo-Jo". For years the trio known as Neon Phoenix dominated the bi-weekly street corner dance competitions in Brooklyn. It seemed like they would reign supreme, unmated by any other group's attempts to outshine them. When Jo-Jo is mysteriously kidnapped by a rival dance troupe, it's up to Darko and Smiles to strut their stuff and save their friend.

    Pestilence

    The plague is upon you. Race to board the last boat leaving Englandia - a forsaken land that is crumbling from the Black Death. Levy your strength by choosing from a selection of provincial characters such as the Plague Doctor, the Knight, or the Hedgewitch, among others. It'll be every person for themselves at the End of the World.

    Voyage

You oversee the first live voyage to the Red Planet - a months long journey that will push you and your crew to the limits. Can you survive the unexplored nature of space travel?

    EDUC 765 - Certificate Course Design Documents

    My project utilizes a community-based effort to increase public perception and participation in local recycling programs. The project's final form will be a flexible online course meant to inform learners of the importance of local recycling efforts, the global plastic crisis, and practical ways to improve a household's waste management habits. The course will initially focus on the community members, aged 16+, that are living in Dunn County, Wisconsin. Although Dunn County will be the focus group, the materials could be updated to fit any locality with a nearby recycling facility.
